Workspaces (also called organizations) allow you to manage multiple publications or projects from a single Marble account. Each workspace is completely isolated with its own content, team, settings, and billing.

## What is a workspace?

A workspace is an isolated environment that contains:

- **Content** - Posts, categories, tags, and authors
- **Media library** - Uploaded images, videos, and documents
- **Team members** - Users with assigned roles and permissions
- **API configuration** - API keys with scoped access
- **Webhooks** - Event-driven integrations
- **Billing** - Subscription and usage tracking

<Info>
  Think of workspaces as separate publications or projects. For example, you might have one workspace for your company blog and another for your documentation site.
</Info>

## Creating a workspace

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access workspace settings">
    Click your profile in the sidebar, then select **Create workspace**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter workspace details">
    Provide a name, slug, and optional description for your workspace.
    
    - **Name** - Display name for your workspace (e.g., "Company Blog")
    - **Slug** - URL-friendly identifier (e.g., "company-blog")
    - **Description** - Optional details about the workspace
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure settings">
    Set your timezone and optionally upload a logo.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create workspace">
    Click **Create** to set up your new workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The slug must be unique across all Marble workspaces and cannot be changed after creation.
</Note>

## Team members

Workspaces support collaboration with role-based access control.

### User roles

Marble has three built-in roles:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Owner">
    Full control over the workspace:
    
    - Manage all content and settings
    - Invite and remove team members
    - Assign roles to members
    - Delete the workspace
    - Access billing and subscription
    
    <Note>
      Each workspace has exactly one owner. Ownership can be transferred to another member.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Admin">
    Administrative access to most features:
    
    - Create, edit, and delete posts
    - Manage categories, tags, and authors
    - Upload and manage media
    - Invite team members (cannot change roles)
    - Configure webhooks and API keys
    
    Cannot access billing or delete the workspace.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Member">
    Basic content creation access:
    
    - Create and edit their own posts
    - Upload media
    - View categories, tags, and authors
    
    Cannot manage team members, webhooks, or API keys.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Inviting team members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to team settings">
    Go to **Settings** > **Members** in your workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click invite button">
    Click **Invite member** to open the invitation form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter email and role">
    Provide the team member's email address and select their role (Admin or Member).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send invitation">
    Click **Send invitation**. The recipient will receive an email with instructions to join.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Invited users must have a Marble account. If they don't have one, they'll be prompted to sign up.
</Info>

### Managing invitations

Pending invitations are displayed in the **Invitations** section. You can:

- **Resend** - Send the invitation email again
- **Revoke** - Cancel the invitation before it's accepted
- **View status** - See pending, accepted, or expired invitations

Invitations expire after 7 days and must be resent if not accepted.

### Removing team members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to members list">
    Go to **Settings** > **Members**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the member">
    Locate the team member you want to remove.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove member">
    Click the menu icon (⋯) next to their name and select **Remove member**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm removal">
    Confirm the action. The member will immediately lose access to the workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Removing a team member does not delete their content. Posts they created remain in the workspace.
</Warning>

## Leaving a workspace

If you're a team member (not the owner), you can leave a workspace:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access team settings">
    Go to **Settings** > **Members**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click leave workspace">
    Click **Leave workspace** at the bottom of the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm departure">
    Confirm that you want to leave. You'll lose access immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Workspace owners cannot leave their workspace. You must transfer ownership to another member first.
</Note>

## Switching workspaces

If you belong to multiple workspaces:

1. Click the workspace name in the sidebar
2. Select the workspace you want to switch to
3. The dashboard will reload with the selected workspace's content

Your active workspace is persisted across sessions.

## Deleting a workspace

<Warning>
  Deleting a workspace is permanent and cannot be undone. All content, media, and settings will be permanently deleted.
</Warning>

Only workspace owners can delete a workspace:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to general settings">
    Go to **Settings** > **General**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scroll to danger zone">
    Find the **Delete workspace** section at the bottom.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm deletion">
    Enter the workspace name to confirm and click **Delete workspace**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Before deletion, Marble will:

- Remove all team members
- Cancel active subscriptions
- Delete all content and media
- Revoke all API keys
- Remove all webhooks
